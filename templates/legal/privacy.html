Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Quiet Build ("we," "us," or "our") operates a social-media publishing and scheduling tool (the "Service") that lets an account owner connect their own social media accounts and create, schedule, and publish content to them. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Service you agree to this Policy.

Plain-language summary: We only collect what we need to publish content to the social accounts you connect. We never sell your data, never post without your instruction, and you can disconnect any account or delete your data at any time.

Who we are

The Service is operated by Quiet Build (a sole operator based in Roswell, Georgia, USA). For any privacy question or request, contact jrwalker502@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account & authorization data. When you connect a social platform (for example TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Bluesky, Pinterest), we receive OAuth access and refresh tokens and basic profile information such as your username, display name, avatar, and account ID. Tokens are stored encrypted and are used only to act on that account at your direction.
  • Content you create. The captions, titles, media (images and videos), scheduling times, and per-post settings (such as privacy level and comment/duet/stitch preferences) that you enter to publish a post.
  • Platform content we retrieve at your request. To power features you use, we may retrieve data from a connected account, such as post performance metrics and inbox items (comments or messages), so you can view them in one place.
  • Usage & technical data. Basic logs (timestamps, actions, errors) needed to operate, secure, and debug the Service.

How we use information

  • To publish, schedule, and manage the content you explicitly ask us to post to your connected accounts.
  • To display your posts, analytics, and inbox items back to you inside the Service.
  • To maintain valid authorization (for example, refreshing expired tokens) so the Service keeps working.
  • To secure, troubleshoot, and improve the Service.

We do not sell your personal information, use it for advertising, or publish anything to your accounts except at your explicit direction.

How we share information

We share information only as needed to run the Service:

  • The social platforms you connect. When you publish or retrieve content, we send the necessary data to that platform's API (for example, uploading your video and its settings to TikTok). Your use of each platform is also governed by that platform's own terms and privacy policy.
  • Infrastructure providers (subprocessors). We host the Service on Railway and store media files on Cloudflare R2. These providers process data only to provide hosting and storage to us.

We may disclose information if required by law.

TikTok data

When you connect a TikTok account, we use TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API solely to (a) confirm your identity and posting eligibility (via TikTok's creator_info endpoint) and (b) publish the specific video you choose, with the privacy level and interaction settings you select. We handle TikTok user data in accordance with the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and TikTok's Developer Guidelines. We do not use TikTok data for any purpose other than providing the posting features you request, and we delete stored TikTok tokens when you disconnect the account.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data only as long as your account is connected and you use the Service. You can:

  • Disconnect any platform at any time — this revokes and deletes the stored authorization tokens for that account.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing jrwalker502@gmail.com; we will delete it within 30 days.

Security

Authorization tokens are encrypted at rest, traffic is served over HTTPS, and access is restricted. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw authorization at any time by disconnecting an account. To exercise any right, email jrwalker502@gmail.com.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date on this page.
